Welcome to Geekout.

Geekout is a geek co-working space where geeks can work and collaborate on their personal or community projects. We started our first geekpad at Lokhandwala Andheri which was the only 24hr operating co-working space with in-house mini kitchen and unlimited coffee to boost your operations. We also host small hackathons and geekcamps at the place.

The second Geekout Zone is on Link Road Andheri West near Laxmi Industrial Estate and can host 30+ geeks and capacity to hold unlimited geekness.The Zone has a meeting room, one private cabin and single desk with high speed internet.

Geekout just don't doesn't provide space to work but to grow, Its the only coworking place in Mumbai to provide:

  • Talent Pool Connect to any technology requirement of a start up.
  • Connect with Tech Consultant to solve your any technological issues you face in your project or startup.
  • A "special freelancer plan" were they provide leads to co working freelancers.
  • Special discount for hackathons and workshops organised by Codeniti and CodeGurukul.
  • Goodies like Free AWS Credits, Free Github Repos, Free Coffee and so on are just another perks at any geek coworking place.
