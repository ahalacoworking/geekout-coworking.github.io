Welcome to Geekout.

Geekout is a geek co-working space where geeks can work and collaborate on their personal or community projects. We started our first geekpad at Lokhandwala Andheri which was the only 24hr operating co-working space with in-house mini kitchen and unlimited coffee to boost your operations. We also host small hackathons and geekcamps at the venue.

The second Geekout Zone is on Link Road Andheri West near Laxmi Industrial Estate and can host 30+ geeks and capacity to hold unlimited geekness.The Zone has a meeting room, one private cabin and single desk with high speed internet.

Geekout just doesn't provide space to work but to grow. Its the only coworking place in Mumbai to provide:

  • Talent Pool Connect to any technology requirement of a start up.
  • Connect with Tech Consultants to solve any technology related issues you face in your project or startup.
  • A "special freelancer plan" where we provide 10+ leads every week( avg. worth of 4000 USD per lead) to co working freelancers.
  • Special discount for hackathons and workshops organised by Codeniti and CodeGurukul.
  • Goodies like Free AWS Credits, Free Github Repos, Free Coffee and so on are available at our geek coworking place.
